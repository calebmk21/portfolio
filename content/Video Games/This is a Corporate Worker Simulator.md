## Project Overview
The GMTK 2025 Game Jam had the theme "Loop" which could be interpreted in many ways. My team had the idea of using the "loop" to be a representation of what corporate life can feel like. The voices in your head, represented by four of the seven deadly sins, will attempt to sway you one way or another. Will you be a good little employee and keep working or will you risk it all for an extra five minutes of sleep? Or perhaps a hidden third, fourth or fifth option...




## My Role
 I was the lead programmer and worked on the game manager and implemented the dialogue system using YarnSpinner. I'm also the voice of Sloth and Pride.
