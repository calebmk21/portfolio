I love Dungeons and Dragons. I’ve been playing 5th edition since 2019, and have been a dungeon master since 2020. I use DMing as a creative outlet to create compelling narratives for my players. Worldbuilding for D&D is by *far* my favorite writing medium. Being able to create an interactive worlds for 

something something escapism lol
also i always homebrew my own worlds. I’ve never run a premade campaign because I’m just like that. 

> ***A brief special thanks***
> It’s impossible to talk about D&D without a shoutout to my wonderful players. It’s because of them that I get to even be a dungeon master, weaving my world around their actions. I may have created the world, but they are truly the ones to shape it. Their legacy is up to them, as is how they choose to overcome the challenges I throw at them. I get to hone my craft, especially as they challenge me with the bane of all DMs: cheese. No matter how infrequently we meet, I know we always will find time to have another session. I also wanted to use this space to give another special thanks to my lovely girlfriend, who helps me flesh out this world, its inhabitants, and the themes of my campaign. You’ve left a mark on my worlds <3
