I love Dungeons and Dragons. I’ve been playing 5th edition since 2019, and have been a dungeon master since 2020. I use DMing as a creative outlet to create compelling narratives for my players. Worldbuilding for D&D is by *far* my favorite writing medium. I especially love being able to create interactive worlds for my friends to explore. Seeing them light up when they uncover new lore or finally solve a puzzle is what keeps me going as a dungeon master.

## Current Campaign
I am currently the DM for one campaign titled "Reflections" set in my homebrew world [[Faltera]]. The party consists of an Arcane Trickster Rogue named Nim, Oath of the Watchers Paladin named Sarah, an Aberrant Mind Sorcerer / Genie Warlock named Bloo, and  Blood Cleric named Yul. This band of misfits has gotten themselves noticed internationally as the group who helped save the material plane by stopping a ritual to summon an extraplanar being. Currently, they've traveled across the ocean to slay a rogue aberration who is currently terrorizing our paladin's home town.
